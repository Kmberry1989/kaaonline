Artworks Gallery

Our retail co-op gallery in downtown Kokomo features member art, receptions, and studio spaces for rent.

Located in beautiful Highland Park, the Art Center houses our Permanent Collection and hosts classes, competitions, and camps. Be sure to visit the new **Little Free Art Library** out front!

Tue-Sat: 1 PM - 4 PM (Mar-Nov)

About KAA

The Kokomo Art Association was organized in 1926. Bylaws and a constitution were established in 1962, and not-for-profit status was attained in 1981. We are dedicated to promoting the arts and supporting local artists in our community.
