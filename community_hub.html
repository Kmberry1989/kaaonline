Artist Alley Project

12th Annual Call for Entries - Artist Alley Project 2025

Project Description

In 2012, the Kokomo Art Association and the Greater Kokomo Downtown Association launched a collaborative effort to revitalize a forgotten alley in downtown Kokomo. This initiative transformed the alley into a vibrant outdoor gallery space, showcasing local artists' mural-style works on panels. The Artist Alley Project provides a unique opportunity for artists to display their creativity publicly and contributes to the beautification and cultural vibrancy of downtown Kokomo.

The Project is now in its 12th year, with plans to replace 12 existing panels with new works. Artists are invited to submit designs for these 4’x8’ panels.

Submission Details

  • Submission Deadline: September 15, 2024
  • Panel Size: 4’ (height) x 8’ (width)
  • Number of Panels to be Replaced: 12
  • Theme: Your design should reflect the spirit of community, art, or Kokomo. Abstract, representational, or any other style is welcome, as long as it is family-friendly and suitable for public display.
  • Media: Designs will be reproduced as vinyl wraps to be applied to the aluminum panels. Therefore, original art or high-resolution digital designs are acceptable.

About KAA

The Kokomo Art Association was organized in 1926. Bylaws and a constitution were established in 1962, and not-for-profit status was attained in 1981. We are dedicated to promoting the arts and supporting local artists in our community.
