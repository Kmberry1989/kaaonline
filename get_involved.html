Volunteer With Us

The Kokomo Art Association relies on the dedication of our amazing volunteers to help us run our galleries, assist with events, support art classes, and contribute to the overall success of our community programs. Whether you are an artist, an art lover, or simply someone looking to give back, there are many ways you can make a difference. We are always looking for passionate individuals to help us with various tasks and events. Whether you can spare a few hours a month or want to be a regular contributor, we have a place for you.
